Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa. It is majorly made up of three ethnic groups namely: Igbo, Hausa, and Yoruba. Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa and is referred to the Giant of Africa. The attached picture is a huge rock located in Niger State which is in the Northern part of Nigeria. It is called the Zuma Rock. The Zuma Rock is is about 300 meters (980 feet) above the ground and a site for tourists who come to behold the massiveness of the rock. Amongst many things that make Nigeria beautiful, it is a country with interesting arts and culture.
